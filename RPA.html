Automation Bot for Attendance Management

A powerful RPA-based solution that automates the collection, analysis, and reporting of attendance data from Excel files using Automation Anywhere. It saves time and eliminates manual errors in repetitive attendance tracking.

Problem It Solves

Manual attendance tracking from Excel sheets is time-consuming and error-prone. This bot ensures fast, reliable, and automated attendance processing, reducing administrative burden.

Features

  • Reads Excel files from a specific folder automatically
  • Validates and processes daily attendance data
  • Generates weekly summary reports for managers
  • Emails the final attendance report to concerned staff

Technologies Used

Automation Anywhere, Excel, Email Automation, File Handling
