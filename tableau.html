During the pandemic, data became critical to inform governments, healthcare systems, and citizens. Analyzing COVID-19 data helps in:

  • Tracking and predicting case surges
  • Comparing regional healthcare responses
  • Identifying high-risk areas for better resource allocation
  • Creating awareness through visual storytelling

Tableau is one of the most powerful tools for data visualization in data science. In this project, it was used for:

  • Real-time data visualizations with filters and drill-down capabilities
  • Interactive dashboards showing confirmed, recovered, and death counts
  • Geo-maps to show regional distribution of cases
  • Trend analysis using time-series charts for monthly and weekly patterns
  • User-friendly interface for non-technical users to explore insights
